 Animal Tales Kids Support

Welcome to the support page for Animal Tales Kids.

Animal Tales Kids is a fun learning app for young children that includes:
- Animal sounds
- A-Z vocabulary learning
- Short moral stories
- Simple kids games

## Need Help?

If you have any questions, feedback, or technical issues, please contact us:

**Email:** support@online247now.com

We aim to respond within 3 business days.

## When contacting support, please include:
- Your device model
- iPhone or iPad version
- A short description of the issue
- Screenshots, if possible

## Common Issues
- Animal sounds not playing
- Story voice not working
- App not opening correctly
- Questions about app features

## Privacy
If you have questions about privacy or data handling, please contact us at the support email above.

## About the App
Animal Tales Kids is designed for children ages 2 to 8 and offers a simple, colorful, and family-friendly learning experience.
