# Privacy Policy for Animal Tales Kids

Effective date: April 3, 2026

Animal Tales Kids is a children's educational app designed for ages 2 to 8. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how families can contact us.

## Information We Collect

Animal Tales Kids does not require account creation or sign-in.

At this time, the app does not intentionally collect personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data
- Photos
- Contacts
- Payment information

The app is designed to work offline for core learning features, including animal sounds, stories, vocabulary learning, and games.

## Children's Privacy

Animal Tales Kids is made for children and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that personal information has been provided to us by a child, please contact us and we will review the request promptly.

## How the App Works

Animal Tales Kids includes:

- Animal learning with sounds and names
- Beginner English learning content
- Short moral stories with voice playback
- Simple mini games for kids

These features are available without requiring a user account.

## Third-Party Services

At the time of this version, Animal Tales Kids does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party sign-in services inside the app.

If a future version adds services such as analytics, ads, subscriptions, or cloud-based content, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

## Data Storage

The app may store simple on-device preferences or learning progress locally on the device to improve the user experience. This information stays on the device and is not used to identify a person.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Because the app does not intentionally collect personal information in this version, there is no personal information shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app and its content safe. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
